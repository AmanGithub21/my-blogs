Describing Heaven

***

      It's the most beautiful moment I have ever lived. In that particular moment I did not care about what I am, what I am not. It was just there in the moment like my adulting has met innocent childhood, suddenly all the impurity vanished, everything was beautiful. Big words but truly honest.

    We were on our way to a waterfall, final destination of our trip. Last minute, change of plan and we went kayaking. After we had put on our lifejackets, we went swimming in the lake. Refreshing. And then it was time for kayaking. The instructor explained everything and we went in. We burned our energy by racing to the middle of the lake so we laid down on our Kayakis. Out of breath, feets in the deep lake, we relaxed. That's when I witnessed the moment which I would remember the rest of my life.

    We were surrounded by the hills when clouds begin to fall down on us. In a moment, we were in the clouds and the visibility become zero. Excited and scared, we begin to shout to know if everyone was ok. Everyone was. Then rainfall suprised us. In a cold weather, little water droplets were magical, felt so soothing. Describing in words is little difficult for a person like me who have very little vocabulary. Our feet in the water made us feel light. our burden, failur, disappointments were all gone in that moment.

"That was the first and only time when I have experienced heaven."

***
